# Activities Reference

This document defines all known Neopets activities tracked by the extension.
It is a **human-readable reference** intended for planning, notes, and edge cases.
Nothing in this file implies automation.

## Seasonal & Time-Window Activities

### Advent Calendar
- URL: https://www.neopets.com/winter/adventcalendar.phtml
- Timing: Once per day
- Availability: December 1 – December 31
- Notes: Should be hidden outside event window

### Snowager
- URL: https://www.neopets.com/winter/snowager.phtml
- Availability Windows:
  - 6–7 AM NPT
  - 2–3 PM NPT
  - 10–11 PM NPT
- Notes:
  - Available once per day
  - Freely available all day from Dec 1 – Jan 3

### Deadly Dice
- URL: https://www.neopets.com/worlds/deadlydice.phtml
